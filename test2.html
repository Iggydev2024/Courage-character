About Courage the Cowardly Dog

Courage the cowardly dog is the pet dog of an old couple who live in a farmhouse in the middle of Nowhere, a fictional town in Kansas. Courage and his owners, Eustace and Muriel and plagued by strange and spooky creatures who invade their house and attempt to finish them off. Despite Courage being a cowardly dog, he is able to protect and defend his owners each time, especially his beloved Muriel who has a soft spot for him.

Courage's Family

Courage is the pet dog to an old couple, Muriel and Eustace. There are many theories explaining the dynamic their relationship with fans suggesting that he is a reincarnation of a son they had who died. This explains why the couple never had any children. Courage is loved by Muriel but has a strained relationship with Eustace who often mistreats and ridicules him.
